Mock Imaging

In an effort to compare observer and theorist's different methods of measuring galaxies size-mass relationship, I have been creating mock observation FIRE dwarf galaxies and measuring them in various different methods. This project has two main goals: to identify any discremancies between different methods of measuring size and mass of galaxies, and to compare the dwarf galaxies produced in FIRE simulations to observed dwarf galaxies.
